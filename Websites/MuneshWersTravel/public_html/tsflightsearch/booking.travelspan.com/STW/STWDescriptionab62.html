Wellness
Escape to the beautiful white sand beaches of Punta Cana with a stay at the all-inclusive Be Live Grand Punta Cana Hotel. Located on famous Bavaro Beach, the Be Live Grand is the perfect destination to enjoy the tranquil Caribbean waters and sublime tropical weather in a relaxed and laid-back atmosphere. Featuring a fusion of Colonial and Caribbean architecture and style, the hotel offers spacious accommodations with lush gardens and superior amenities including friendly, attentive personalized service. Dining options at the Be Live Grand Punta Cana take guests on a global culinary journey. Enjoy a stroll through the lush gardens, lounge on the pristine beach, or delight in a variety of land and water activities. Guests can enjoy a relaxing family holiday, a romantic getaway or a fun vacation with friends, and all will experience the vacation of a lifetime.
